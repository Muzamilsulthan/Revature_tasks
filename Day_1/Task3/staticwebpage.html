Revolution 2020


About Book

Revolution 2020 by Chetan Bhagat takes us on a gripping journey through an India battling corruption, love, and ambition. Set against the backdrop of Varanasi, a city known for its ancient traditions and cultural richness, Bhagat entwines the lives of three individuals - Gopal, Raghav, and Aarti - who find themselves caught in a web of choices that will shape their destinies. As they navigate through the treacherous paths of greed, power, and sacrifice, Revolution 2020 explores the harsh realities of a corrupt system, while shedding light on the delicate complexities of young love and the pursuit of success. Bhagat's masterful storytelling and insightful portrayal of his characters will leave readers captivated, compelled to unravel the tangled threads of this thought-provoking tale.

About Author

Chetan Bhagat, a renowned Indian author, is recognized for his captivating storytelling and his ability to address social issues through his fiction. Born on April 22, 1974, in New Delhi, Bhagat pursued a successful career in investment banking before embarking on his writing journey. His novels, including the bestsellers "Five Point Someone," "One Night @ the Call Center," and "2 States," have captivated audiences with their relatable characters and engaging narratives. Known for his easy-to-read writing style, Bhagat has become a prominent voice in modern Indian literature, seamlessly blending entertainment with thought-provoking themes.
